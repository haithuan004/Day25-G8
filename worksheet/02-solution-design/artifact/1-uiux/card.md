## 1. Giai phap la gi?

Giao dien doi tu "mot doan summary tu tin" thanh bang claim co bang chung. Moi nhan dinh ve ky nang/kinh nghiem se di kem badge `Da co bang chung` hoac `Can review tay`. Khi do tin cay thap, he thong khong hien nut shortlist nhanh ma uu tien nut `Mo CV goc` va `Chuyen manual review`.

## 2. Vi sao sua o lop giao dien?

- Rui ro nay xay ra dung khoanh khac recruiter doc output va quyet dinh co mo CV goc hay khong.
- UI la lop chan cuoi neu parser/prompt van sot loi.

## 4. Tac dung phu

**Co the gay van de gi?**

- Recruiter cam thay them buoc va cham hon khi loc hang loat.
- Man hinh nhieu thong tin hon, neu lam kem se roi.
- Badge `Can review tay` co the bi lam dung qua muc va tao bottleneck.

**Nhom giam van de do bang cach nao?**

- Chi bat buoc claim-level evidence voi output screening risk cao, khong ap dung cho moi note nho.
- Mac dinh chi mo 2-3 claim quan trong nhat; phan con lai an sau `Xem them`.
- Nut manual review chi hien khi confidence gate thap hoac parser loi, khong phai moi truong hop.
